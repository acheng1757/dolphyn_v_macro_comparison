NG End Use Production-Share Allocation

NG End Use emissions are split across fossil/synthetic/bio/ethylene-byproduct NG in proportion to each source's ground-truth share of total NG production (fossil upstream edge, synthetic S-NG plant output, bioenergy NG production, and Ethylene sector NG byproduct production). The four allocated volumes always sum back to the tracked total NG End Use demand.
